The Context

A static resume is not enough.

A resume PDF is still useful, but it does not show your projects, links, updates, or the full picture of your work.

Always current: update your profile once and keep your public presence up to date.

Clear engagement data: see visits, QR scans, and profile activity when analytics are enabled.

Why Now?

People increasingly judge opportunities, follow-up, and fit based on what they can quickly review online. A strong public profile makes that easier.
