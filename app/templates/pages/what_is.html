The Platform Explained

What Is DigiBioFi?

DigiBioFi is a professional identity platform that replaces the static resume with a living, shareable digital profile — complete with QR codes, real-time analytics, and a public URL you can put anywhere.

Your profile, always up to date

A traditional resume is a frozen snapshot. The moment you add a new role, project, or skill, every printed or sent copy is out of date. DigiBioFi gives you one living URL — update once, and everyone who has your link or QR code sees your latest information instantly.

Add experience, showcase projects, list certifications, and write a bio that actually represents who you are today — not who you were three years ago.

QR Code Identity

Every DigiBioFi profile comes with a permanent QR code — automatically generated and always pointing to your current profile. Print it on a business card, add it to a presentation slide, or display it at a conference booth.

When someone scans it, they land on your full profile with everything they need to know about you — no app download required, no friction.

Real analytics, not guesswork

With an Elite plan, you can see how your profile is performing with total views, unique visitors, QR scans, resume downloads, and recent visit activity. That gives you a practical signal for when your profile is getting traction after an application, referral, or networking event.

Use that data to decide when to follow up, which materials are getting attention, and where to focus your networking energy.
