Privacy Policy

Effective April 4, 2026

What we collect

DigiBioFi stores the account information, profile content, uploads, analytics events, and billing identifiers needed to operate your professional identity profile.

How we use it

We use account and profile data to render public profiles, power dashboard editing, generate QR codes, and provide analytics. Payment processing is handled through Stripe when billing is enabled.

Uploads and analytics

Uploaded assets such as profile images, resumes, project thumbnails, QR codes, and certificates are stored to support your profile. Public profile visits, scans, and downloads may be logged for analytics and abuse prevention.

Billing data

DigiBioFi does not store raw card numbers. Stripe processes subscription payments and may provide customer, subscription, and invoice identifiers back to the app for account state management.

Your controls

You can update profile visibility, edit or delete profile content, and manage billing through the dashboard when those features are enabled on your account.
